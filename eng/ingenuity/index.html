Ingenuity

Ingenuity, (nicknamed Ginny), is an autonomous NASA mission that operated from 2021 to 2024 as part of the Mars 2020 mission. The helicopter reached the planet on the underside of the Perseverance rover, which landed on February 18, 2021. Ingenuity made its maiden flight on April 19, 2021, marking the first powered, controlled extraterrestrial flight of any aircraft
